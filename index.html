Origins of Chinese
and Mesopotamian History-Writing

International Academic Online Conversation

中国和两河流域早期历史书写 : 国际学术线上交谈

Our conversation aims to explore the common and unique features of early history-writing practices in ancient China and Mesopotamia, the two important cradles of our civilization’s preoccupation with recorded past. The event shall be conducted as an open lecture series online, and everybody can join freely by signing up.

早期中国和两河流域是人类历史书写的发祥地，而这次讲座系列的目的是通过自由交谈研究这两个地区历史书写 的共同点和区别。这次讲座系列将在线上举行，所有的人可以报名参加。

What does archaeology say? Comments on the practice of reconstructing of the Shang and Xia dynasties

古史的实地版：谈夏、商王朝历史的考古学重建

This talk focuses on the notions of historicity in ancient Chinese culture as reflected in the specific case of Sima Qian’s Records of the Grand Historiographer. The way it narrates the events related to Xia and Shang dynasties has been confirmed in recent archaeology, which deserves special attention from scholars.

司马迁的《史记》历来是研究商朝历史的最重要文献。最近一百年来的考古学发现证明了原来以为司马迁伪造的信息符合考古学真相。而这些发现让学者改变对于古代文献的态度。
